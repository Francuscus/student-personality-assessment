Welcome, Student!

This assessment will help you understand your personality across five key dimensions:

  • Openness to Experience - Creativity & curiosity
  • Conscientiousness - Organization & dependability
  • Extraversion - Sociability & energy
  • Agreeableness - Cooperation & trust
  • Neuroticism - Emotional sensitivity

About This Assessment

You'll answer 50 questions (10 per trait). There are no right or wrong answers - just be honest about how you typically feel and behave.

Time needed: 10-15 minutes
